Welcome to the JLV Information Portal

The Joint Legacy Viewer (JLV) is a clinical application that provides an integrated,read-only display of health data from Department of Defense (DoD), Department ofVeterans Affairs (VA), and private sector partners in a common data viewer. JLV alsoprovides an integrated, read-only, near real-time view of DoD and VA healthcaredata to benefits administrators to facilitate the processing of Veteransâ€™ claims.
